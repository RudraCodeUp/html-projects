BOOK REVIEW PAGE


Title: The Hobbit

Author:John Ronald Reuel Tolkien

"Adventure", a common word that is used but a descriptive one none the less. The book does not only speak of a hobbit going on an adventure but aims its energy at the reader to take them on an adventure. It might might not sound exciting at the very beginning but to be honestly all books start from a subtle low until they reach an extremely high climax.

Title: To Kill A Mockingbird

Harper Lee

"To Kill a Mockingbird" is a great novel and the best one to start with. It's a story that revolves around a sibling (jem and scout) and their lives which bring some unexpected twist and turns while living with their only single parent i.e. their father Atticus and caretaker Calpurnia.

Title: Where The Light Enters

Sara Donati

This meticulously research story focuses on an obstetrician and general sergeant, and the many obstacles they must overcome, especially Sophia who's half African American. Plus so much more!

Title: A Knight In Shining Armor

Jude Deveraux

Historical romance portraying two souls those were meant to be together forever despite of hundred years of gap... just a fraction of imagination for the people around the world...where two persons has twice fallen in love... leaving all technical, modern, traditional aspects of world aside...

Title: The Greatest Novels of Mark Twain

Mark Twain

Very good

Title: Three Men In A Boat

Jerome K. Jerome

Humorous, antic and comic masterpiece is how I would describe Three Men in a Boat, Jerome K. Jerome really writes wonders!
